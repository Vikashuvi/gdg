GDSC WOW is an annual collaborative event organized by Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) across Tamilnadu. It serves as a platform for students, professionals, and tech enthusiasts to connect, learn, and explore the future of technology.

You can register for the event by clicking on the "Register" button on our website. Ensure you secure your spot early as tickets are limited and available on a first-come, first-served basis.

The event is open to anyone interested in technology, including students, professionals, and industry leaders. Whether you're a tech novice or an expert, there's something for everyone.

We offer three types of tickets:

  • Super Early Bird: ₹99
  • Early Bird: ₹149
  • Regular: ₹199
